I have a persistent problem when visiting almost any place which has a menu: it’s difficult to choose what I want since there are too many options to choose from, especially when the difference between dishes is in the sauce, +- one ingredient and other not quite obvious variables. It’s especially confusing when there’s a time pressure and it feels like the others are in rush, but have to wait for my decision, so oftentimes I just order the same thing which I always do, missing the opportunity to try something new.

I wish there was an app which would allow to filter all these options. For example:

- Hot vs. cold dishes
- Spicy vs. mild
- Meat / fish / eggs / vegetarian / vegan etc. options
