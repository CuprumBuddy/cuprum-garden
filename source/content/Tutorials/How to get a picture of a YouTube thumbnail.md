You will need:
- a link like that: `http://img.youtube.com/vi/[video_id]/maxresdefault.jpg`
- the video id from the video URL (the characters after `?v=`)

Replace `[video_id]` in the link with the actual video id. Open the link, then save the image as usual (first, right click or long tap on the image, then choose ”Save Image” option from the menu).
